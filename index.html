Our Heritage

Established in 1985, Golden Luxury Hotel has been a symbol of refined elegance and world-class hospitality. Nestled in the heart of the city, we blend timeless architecture with contemporary luxuries.

Our specialties include curated gastronomic experiences, a serene award-winning spa, and personalized butler service. Every corner whispers opulence, promising an unforgettable stay for discerning travelers.

From royal banquets to intimate getaways, we redefine comfort. Experience the golden standard of hospitality.
